CONGRATULATIONS!!!



Hello everyone!My name is Anelisa cotzee.
I am a second year student at the university of free state. I love playing/writing music, being in nature, learning about the enneagram (a personality thing), listening to podcasts, being silly, and hanging out with friends.
I also love girls' and women's empowerment, and that is why I am excited to be taking on the role of a Girl powered by freedom. GPBF is a truly influential movement, and I hope I can help it flourish and grow. And not just so that girls and young women like you and I can have the space to show up in as our unique selves, but also so that future generations of girls can be confident that they are, and will always be, enough no matter what the media and societal conditioning tells them.
I am not and expert by any means on empowerment, but I know that when girls and women come together, we can be unstoppable, and I am so excited for GPBF to hold that space for us to be just that.
